Quies.

Your private space to think.

A journal for thinking, written by you, kept by your phone.

The promise

Yours, only.

Every entry you make in Quies stays on your device, encrypted with a key your phone holds. We never see it. There is no Quies account. There is no server we can search.

Privacy Receipt

Inside the app, a chronological ledger shows you every data event Quies has performed on your behalf. The marketing promise is verified in code.

The practice

Four tabs.

Today

A daily question and the small things from your day. Write or speak — your choice.

Echoes

Everything you have written or spoken, chronological. Yours to read back any time.

Reflect

Patterns from your own past, plus a CBT toolkit when you want a structure.

Ask

A reflective companion grounded in your own history. On-device. Not therapy.

If it gets hard

We will not look away.

If you write something heavy, Quies will gently surface help — a crisis line, a place to talk to a person. It will never be behind a paywall. It will never be a popup that takes your words from you.

Quies is not a replacement for a therapist. If you are in immediate danger, please call 911 in the United States, or your local emergency line. For mental health crises, dial 988 (US/Canada).
